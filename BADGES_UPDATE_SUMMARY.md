## ✨ Benefits

### 1. **More Specific Recognition**
Managers can now select a badge that closely matches the achievement, making recognition more meaningful.

### 2. **Broader Coverage**
With 30 badges across 6 categories, there's a badge for almost any type of contribution:
- Technical excellence
- Soft skills
- Personal growth
- Team contributions
- Leadership
- Customer focus

### 3. **Better Engagement**
Employees are more likely to strive for recognition when they see diverse pathways:
- A junior developer sees "Growing Star"
- A shy but excellent coder sees "Problem Solver"
- A supportive team member sees "Helping Hand"
- Everyone has something to aspire to

### 4. **Cultural Alignment**
Badges can be mapped to company values:
- Innovation: 💡  🔬 💫
- Collaboration: 🤝 🙌 🔗
- Excellence: 🌟 💎 ⭐
- Growth: 📈 📚 🌱
