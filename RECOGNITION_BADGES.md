## 🎯 How to Give Badges

### For Admins:
1. Navigate to **Recognition** section
2. Click **"Give a Shout out"** 
3. Select recipient from dropdown
4. Choose badge from 30+ options
5. Write appreciation message
6. Click **"Post Shoutout"**

### Features:
- ✅ Badges appear on "Wall of Fame" (this month)
- ✅ Employees can react with 👏 (claps/cheers)
- ✅ Multiple badges can be given to same person
- ✅ Recognition feeds into leaderboard
- ✅ Visible to entire organization

---

## 📈 Recognition Leaderboard

The system tracks:
- **Total Kudos Count**: Number of badges received
- **Total Cheers**: 👏 reactions from colleagues
- **Top Recognition**: Displayed with 🥇 🥈 🥉 medals
- **Monthly Wall of Fame**: Current month leaders highlighted

## ✨ Why 30 Badges?

The expanded badge set allows for:
- **Specific Recognition**: Match the badge to the actual achievement
- **Diversity**: Recognize different types of contributions
- **Motivation**: Employees see variety of paths to recognition
- **Company Culture**: Reflects organization values across domains
- **Inclusivity**: Everyone can be recognized for something unique
